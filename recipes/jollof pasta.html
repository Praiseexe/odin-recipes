Jollof pasta

Description

Pasta over the years has numerous ways and recipes to making it, this is a popular and fast recipe thats really tasty and super easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (preferably Spaghetti)
  • Tomato Paste
  • Blended pepper mix (Tomato,Red peppers,Onions)
  • Seasoning (according to your preferred mix)
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a fresh clean pot add enough oil to fry your blended pepper mix.
  2. Once the oil is heated pour in your pepper mix and allow fry for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add your tomato paste to the frying pepper mix and allow fry for 2 more minutes.
  4. After 2 minutes add your seasoning and allow fry till the oil begins to float above the pepper mix.
  5. Once the pepper is fried add enough water to boil the pasta and leave on the heat till it begins to boil.
  6. When the water starts to boil add the pasta and allow cook till the water is dry and the pasta is your desired texture.
