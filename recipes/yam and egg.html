Yam and egg

Description

This is a simple great tasting recipe for Yam and egg which is already a simple meal

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil (or preferred option)
  • Chopped pepper and spring onions
  • Preferred seasoning mix (for fried egg.)

Steps

  1. Peel the back off the yam and cut up the desired amount of pieces you want to make.
  2. Wash and rinse off the body of the yam with water multiple times till its clean.
  3. In a clean pot, fill with enough water to boil your desired amount of yam.
  4. Once the water begins to boil put in the cut pieces of yam and add a pinch of salt and a table spoon of oil and let boil till the yam is your desired texture.
  5. In a frying pan add enough oil to fry your desired amount of eggs.
  6. Once the oil is heated pour in your chopped pepper and spring onions and let fry for about 2 minutes.
  7. After letting the pepper fry for a little while pour in your seasoning mix and let fry with the chopped pepper and spring onions till you notice the onions turning golden brown.
  8. Once the yam finally reaches your desired texture turn off the heat and drain out the water and allow sit till the eggs are ready.
  9. Once the onions start turning golden brown pour in your already cracked and beat eggs, mix lightly with the chopped pepper and spring onions and allow fry till its ready.
